First 2 hours
  • Find the emergency folder or family packet.
  • Confirm access to the main phone, main email, and password manager.
  • Call spouse, sibling, trusted friend, CA or CPA, and employer HR if needed.
First 24 hours
  • Locate main bank accounts, insurance cards, property documents, and immigration files.
  • Check which payments cannot stop, such as mortgage, rent, insurance, and utilities.
  • If phone is lost, start SIM replacement and email recovery before banking recovery.
First week
  • Order certified copies of key documents if needed.
  • Contact insurance, retirement account providers, banks, brokers, and lawyers.
  • For India assets, ask the CA about nominee claims, NRO/NRE movement, and required forms.
Lost phone recovery order: email recovery first, phone/SIM next, password manager next, then banks and brokers. Do not start with the most painful bank app if email and OTP access are broken.
